All entries will be preceded with Heading 5 denoting whom the entity speaking is. Narrator will always be listed as Narrator. When the narrator is speaking on behalf of a non player character, the name of the npc with the NPC suffix. A player character's name will include the prefix PC. Anything in quotation marks is said in character, whilst anything outside of this is tabletalk. Change of Scenes are denoted with a Heading 4 and a description of the location and time.

This should look as follows:

##### NPC Veriseth
Runs down the hall, turning the corner at its end.

##### PC Kikh
"Coward"

##### Narrator
The sound echoes down the corridor.

##### PC Kikh
I tread down the hall slowly.
