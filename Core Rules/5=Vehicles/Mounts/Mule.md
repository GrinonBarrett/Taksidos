### Mule
A hybrid of horse and donkey. Because of the vast variety of hybrids, a mule can benefit from a primary or secondary breed type of a horse in addition to the features below.

**Creature Type**: Horses are Beast creatures. 

**Size**: Mules are between 4'2" to 5'8" at the withers. They can weigh between 800 and 1,000 pounds. They are medium creatures.

**Hitpoints**: A mule has 1 hitpoint.

**Attributes**:  
[Body] 16 (+5)  
[Fort] 12 (+4)  
[Rflx] 12 (+4)  
[Will] 10 (+3)  
[Inte] 7 (+2)  
[Prcp] 12 (+4)  
[Empy] 7 (+2)  

**Languages**: Mules don't speak.

**Run Speed**: It may run up to 6 yards per move.

**Swim Speed**: It may swim up to 1 yard per move, presuming the water isn't too rough.

**Weight Load Limits**;  
* Light: 20% mule's wieght.
* Moderate: 10 + ([Body] score)% of mule's weight.
* Heavy: Double ([Body] score)% of mule's weight.

**Unarmed Attacks**;

 • Slam: The mule may use any part of its body to strike a foe within 2 feet. This is a melee bare attack.

 • Stomp: The mule can attempt to stomp a foe within 1 foot, dealing a crushing attack. It has a -1 penalty to its attack, but has a +3 bonus for the purpose of determining if the attack is a critical hit.
