### Bogkin
Grandfolk of the swamps. In their home environment, they are known to be greedy, paranoid, and difficult to deal with. Outside of their home, they are known for their ambition and secrecy.

**Creature Type**: Bogkin are Humanoids.

**Size**: Bogkin stand between 8' and 9'6" tall. They can weigh between 400-650 pounds. They are large creatures.

**Hitpoints**: Bogkin have 3 Hitpoints.

**Attributes**:  
[Body] 20 (+6)  
[Fort] 12 (+4)  
[Rflx] 10 (+3)  
[Will] 10 (+3)  
[Inte] 11 (+3)  
[Prcp] 13 (+4)  
[Empy] 9 (+3)  

**Languages**: Bogkin speak Jotine and another language as appropriate to where they operate.

**Run Speed**: It may run up to 6 yards per move.

**Climb Speed**: It may climb up to 2 yards per move, presuming it is reasonable terrain to climb.

**Swim speed**: It may swim up to 1.5 yards per move, presuming the water isn’t too rough.

**Weapon Attacks**: The bogkin is capable of using weapons. It even has proficiency in two weapons (usually sling and club).

**Defenses**: A bogkin can use armor and a shield.

**Unarmed Attacks**;

 • Unarmed Strike: The bogkin may use part of its body to strike a foe within 2 feet. This is a melee unarmed attack.

#### Variants
Among the bogkin people, there are a few standouts. 

**Bogkin Bogriner**: Some say they are witches, others say they or diviners, and some claim them mediators of magical contracts. Bogriners are capable of performing magic. They typically know the following spells: _Animation_, _Intoxify_, _Rotward_, _Silent Alarm_, _Wretched Ray_, and two more spells. They are also proficient with clubs, daggers, slings, and staves.  
Bogriners are more apt to do business with adventurers, but can be tricky to deal with.

-------------

#### Special Features

**Show of Strength**: When the bogkkin would gain a bonus to their melee attack due to having a higher [Body] score, they may choose to replace their +1 bonus to the attack with a bonus equal to their [Body] bonus. If they do, their foe adds their [Body] bonus to their defense.

**Bogkin Resilieince**: Bogkin double their attribute bonus on all [Fort] and [Inte] saves against magical effects.
