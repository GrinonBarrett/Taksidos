### Ploppar
Colorful slimes that are somehow animated. They are mostly harmless. The plural is ploppain and their collective noun is a smattering.

**Creature Type**: Ploppars are Infigura.

**Size**: Ploppars are about 4"-5" tall and about a foot in length. They can weigh up to 10 pounds. They are diminutive creatures.

**Languages**: Ploppain don't speak.

-----

**Hitpoints**: A Ploppar has a single hitpoint.

**Attributes**:  
[Body] 5 (+1)  
[Fort] 7 (+2)  
[Rflx] 12 (+4)  
[Will] 15 (+5)  
[Inte] 2 (+0)  
[Prcp] 5 (+1)  
[Empy] 5 (+1)  

**Run Speed**: It may run up to 5 yards per move.

**Climb Speed**: It may climb up to 4 yards, presuming it is reasopnable terrain to do so.

**Unarmed Attacks**;

 • Venomous Touch: The ploppar may use any part of its body to strike a foe within 1 foot. This is a melee bare attack.

#### Special Features 

**Amorphous**: Ploppain don’t retain a single cohesive shape but are instead in the form of a glob of gooey material. This confers some strange properties to it.  
 • Immunity to falling and knockback damage.  
 • Obstacles that would reduce the speed of it have only half of their normal effect unless they specifically affect amorphous creatures as well.  
 • All forms of non-magical attack against it have disadvantage.  
 • Anytime a spell would be cast against it that would affect its form, shape, composition, or mental state- it gains a d12 bonus to its saves against that effect.  
 • It cannot be grappled.  
 • While it has a creature grappled, it can treat that creature as being unarmored for the purpose of attacking it.  
 • It is capable of traveling through any opening small enough to stick one’s fist.  
 • Cannot use armor, shields, or any item.   
 • Receives wounds, but no effect from them.

 **Infigural Nature**: Being infigura, ploppain several advantages.  
 • Immunity to all poisons and diseases, including toxic damage, except those that specifically affect infigura.  
 • Immunity to the effects of bloodloss.  
 • Immunity to fatigue from outside sources.  
 • Immunity to sleep.  
 • Immunity to effects which cause asphyxiation.  
 • Immunity to effects which would inflict the prone condition, unless it specifically targets infigura.  
 • Does not require sleep to rest, but must still manage to relax and unwind as other creatures would.  
 • Immunity to incapacitation, but is destroyed when wounded with no remaining hitpoints.

**Minimal Sensing**: It is, in the most technical sense, blind. It is also devoid of the senses of smell and hearing. Instead it relies on sensing vibrations and its ability to taste everything it comes in contact with. Anytime it would attempt a check or save based on perceiving something, it has disadvantage (likewise, it should get advantage against illusions based on such stimuli). If it would rely on multiple missing senses, it suffers an additional -2 penalty for each total sense. If it is touching or tasting something, it does not have any issues identifying it.

**Mindless**: Ploppain do not have capacity for independent thought. As such, they cannot participate in articulate thought or coordination. They are also unaffected by mind-influencing effects and fear-based effects.

#### Gaining a Ploppar Servile

By casting the Animate spell on a mixture of sand or clay, salt, distilled water, a gram of either silver or iron, and a drop of hydorythros, an invoker can create their own ploppar lesser servile. When created this way, it is treated as having two hitpoints.

**Dominion Benefits**: The ploppar gains the benefit as appropriate to your domain.  
* **Chosen Spirit**. It gains advantage on opposed checks.
* **The Earth**. Non-magical, attacks have disadvantage against it. This does not apply to falling or knockback.
* **The Flame**. When the ploppar dies, it can explode- causing a force attack against everything (except yourself) within a number of yards equal to your level and a fire attack against everything within a 3 yards.
* **The Grave**. Undead cannot detect the Ploppar. Necrotic attacks against the ploppar have disadvantage.
* **The Light**: Radiant and Ki attacks have disadvantage against the ploppar.
* **Nobility**. As an action, you may allow the ploppar to sense through one of your sense or vice versa. This continues so long as you concentrate. You incur a point of spell sickness when this ends.
* **The Sea**. You may psychically command it as an action or reaction to be able to automatically float, automatically sink, or neither. It gains a swim speed of 1.5 yards.
* **The Sky**. Its modes of movement are each increased by 1 yard.
* **Valdercaller**. A random spell is chosen when you gain this servile/domain. The ploppar funtions as a staff for the purpose of casting that spell while it is on your person, but only for yourself.
