### Militun
Former Etrid members of the Meolasine army. They have turned their experiences into skills for "independant contracts".

**Creature Type**: Etrids are humanoids.

**Size**: Etrids stand between 4-5 feet tall. They can weigh between 90-180 pounds. They are medium creatures.

**Hitpoints**: A militun has a single hitpoint.

**Attributes**:  
[Body] 13 (+4)  
[Fort] 11 (+3)  
[Rflx] 11 (+3)  
[Will] 11 (+3)  
[Inte] 10 (+3)  
[Prcp] 11 (+3)  
[Empy] 9  (+3)  

**Languages**: Militun often speak the Common Tongue, Meolasine, and one other.

**Run Speed**: They may run up to 4.5 yards per move.

**Climb Speed**: They may climb up to 1.5 yards per move, presuming it is reasonable terrain to climb.

**Swim speed**: They may swim up to 2.5 yards per move, presuming the water isn’t too rough.

**Weapon Attacks**: The militun is capable of using weapons. It even has proficiency in axes and swords and specialization in shields and javelins. Furthermore, its total level of training in one of those is increased by one.

**Defenses**: A militun can use certain armor and a shield. It uses the standard attack chart as appropriate to its armor and/or shield for the etrid species.

**Unarmed Attacks**;

 • Unarmed Strike: Its unarmed attacks may be treated as cutting attacks.

**Rough Hide**: Etrids cannot use armor unless it states otherwise. When unarmored, an etrid is treated as wearing Gambeson.

**Stiff Joints**: Any sprint action it takes requires it take a level of fatigue.

**Primal Instincts**: As an action, it may enter a frenzied state. While in this state;  
 • Itu gain a +3 bonus to [Body] checks and saves, +2 to [Will] checks and saves, and +1 bonus to [Rflx] checks and saves.  
 • Its rough hide provides defense like Brigindine.  
 • Anytime it performs an [Inte] check or save, roll a d8. This is a penalty suffered to the check/save.  
 • It ignores your Stiff Joints feature.  
This frenzy lasts for 2d6+3 rounds.  
Once the frenzy ends, it suffers two levels of exhaustion.

**Venomous Bite**: As an additional option when it succeeda a critical success while brawling, it may bite the foe whom it is brawling. This is treated as a biting attack (ignoring the penalty for the attack while brawling). If it hits, the victim must succeed a DV 15 [Fort] save or lose 1 hitpoint as a result of the venom. An additional save must be made at the end of every round until two successful saves are made. Each failure has the venom reduce its hitpoints by 1.  
An etrid gains a +5 bonus to their saves against an etrid’s bite.
