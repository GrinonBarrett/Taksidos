### Shield Bearer
A tradition in both Lithenic and Emerian cultures, the shield bearer is a squire who carries weapons for their employer on the battlefied and is also well versed in the use of shield and typically another weapon. Though not often elite soldiers themselves, they can be expected to protect another on the field of battle and remain alert. In Emeria, the role of being a lord's shield bearer is one of great honor.

**Creature Type**: Shield bearers are Humanoids, though hypothetically they could be of other types (see below).

**Size**: Shield Bearers are between 4.5-6 feet tall. They can weigh between 90 and 185 pounds. They are medium creatures.

**Hitpoints**: A shield bearer has no hitpoints.

**Attributes**:  
[Body] 10 (+3)  
[Fort] 10 (+3)  
[Rflx] 11 (+3)  
[Will] 11 (+3)  
[Inte] 10 (+3)  
[Prcp] 11 (+3)  
[Empy] 10 (+3)  

**Languages**: Shield bearers often speak the Common Tongue and one other.

**Run Speed**: It may run up to 5 yards per move.

**Swim speed**: It may swim up to 2 yards per move, presuming the water isn’t too rough.

**Weapon Attacks**: The shield bearer is capable of using weapons. It even has proficiency in one weapon and specialization in shields (as though it had the Warrior's Shield Proficiency feature). Furthermore, it also has either expertise or Warrior Mastery with a shield.

**Defenses**: A shield bearer can use armor and a shield. The shield bearer uses the normal attack charts appropriate to its armor and/or shield.

**Unarmed Attacks**;

 • Unarmed Strike: The shield bearer use part of its body to strike a foe within 1 foot. This is a melee bare attack.
