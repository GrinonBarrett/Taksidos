### Gladekin
Grandfolk of the woods. The reclusive gladekin live a primitive life in which they seek to maintain harmony with nature in every aspect of life. For the most part, they can be considered the gentlest of the grandfolk- but those that live near ever encroaching settlements find their hostilities much quicker.

**Creature Type**: Gladekin are Humanoids.

**Size**: Galdekin stand between 7'9" and 9' tall. They can weigh between 400-600 pounds. They are large creatures.

**Hitpoints**: Gladekin have 3 Hitpoints.

**Attributes**:  
[Body] 20 (+6)  
[Fort] 12 (+4)  
[Rflx] 11 (+3)  
[Will] 10 (+3)  
[Inte] 10 (+3)  
[Prcp] 12 (+4)  
[Empy] 10  (+3)  

**Languages**: Gladekin speak Jotine and another language as appropriate to where they operate.

**Run Speed**: It may run up to 6.5 yards per move.

**Climb Speed**: It may climb up to 1.5 yards per move, presuming it is reasonable terrain to climb.

**Swim speed**: It may swim up to 1.5 yards per move, presuming the water isn’t too rough.

**Weapon Attacks**: The gladekin is capable of using weapons. It even has proficiency in two weapons (usually bow and club).

**Defenses**: A gladekin can use armor and a shield.

**Unarmed Attacks**;

 • Unarmed Strike: The gladekin may use part of its body to strike a foe within 2 feet. This is a melee bare attack.

#### Variants
Among the yardkin people, there are a few standouts. 

**Gladekin Rangers**: Gladekin rangers have additional training in weapons. Their normal weapon proficiencies are supplemented by proficiency in bows, clubs, spears, pikes, javelins, brusgiata, and slings.

**Gladekin Wardens**: Keepers of the wood. Wardens are capable of performing magic. They typically know the following spells: _Expulsion Sphere_, _Green Ward_, _Thornvalder_, _Oakvalder_, and two more spells. They are also proficient with bows, brusgiata, and clubs in addition to their normal proficiencies.

#### Special Features

**Show of Strength**: When the gladekin would gain a bonus to their melee attack due to having a higher [Body] score, they may choose to replace their +1 bonus to the attack with a bonus equal to their [Body] bonus. If they do, their foe adds their [Body] bonus to their defense.

**Yardkin Resilieince**: Yardkin double their attribute bonus on all [Fort] and [Rflx] saves against magical effects.
