### Herporcianary
Frog-boar-man hybrids who serve Grailoustre. Though many are malicious in nature, most are simply worn by the weight of their duty. They are made by some profane method known only to Grailoustre. The plural is herporcianaries.

**Creature Type**: Herporcianaries are bigenerae and are affected by magic as though they were monsters.

**Size**: Herporcianaries are between 5'-5'7" tall. They can weigh between 130 and 160 pounds. They are medium creatures.

**Hitpoints**: A herporcianary has 1 hitpoint.

**Attributes**:  
[Body] 13 (+4)  
[Fort] 10 (+3)  
[Rflx] 12 (+4)  
[Will] 11 (+3)  
[Inte] 10 (+3)  
[Prcp] 12 (+4)  
[Empy] 9  (+3)  

**Languages**: Herporcianaries often speak the Wild Tongue and Gobline.

**Run Speed**: It may run up to 5 yards per move.

**Swim Speed**: It may swim up to 2 yards per move, presuming the water isn’t too rough.

**Climb Speed**: It may climb up to 2 yards per round  presuming it is reasonable terrain to do so.

**Weapon Attacks**: The herporcianary is capable of using weapons. It even has specialization in clubs and proficiency in another weapon.

**Defenses**: A herporcianary can use armor and a shield. It uses the standard attack chart as appropriate to its armor and/or shield. Typically, they wear gambeson but may wear cuirass instead.

**Unarmed Attacks**;

 • Unarmed Strike: The herporcianary may use part of its body to strike a foe within 1 foot. This is a melee bare attack.

**Skill Proficiencies**: The herporcenary has proficiency in Athletics or Wrestling, and another skill.

**Fine Flip**: In place of performing an attack (by action or reaction), it may perform a fine flip. Treat as a jump whose maximum distance is increased by a number of feet equal to its [Body] score. For the purpose of falling as a result, it ignores a number of feet equal to its [Body] score. It must perform a rest before doing so again.

**Hasty**: The herporcianary rolls an additional d6 when rolling for initiative and gets a +1 bonus to attacks against foes with a later turn order than them.
