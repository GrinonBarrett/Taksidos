### Velociraptor
One of the more common varieties of what some would call dinosaurs, they are known to roam the woods of the emerian borders.

**Creature Type**: Velociraptors are Reptiles.

**Size**: Velociraptor's stand between 1'5" and 1'7" and are between 4'11" and 6'10" in length. The weight is usually between 31 and 43 pounds. They are small creatures.

**Hitpoints**: A velociraptor has no hitpoints.

**Attributes**:  
[Body] 7  (+2)  
[Fort] 9  (+3)  
[Rflx] 13 (+4)  
[Will] 9  (+3)  
[Inte] 10 (+3)  
[Prcp] 11 (+3)  
[Empy] 9 (+3)  

**Languages**: Velociraptors don't speak, but can convey very basic information by barking or chirping.

**Run Speed**: It may run up to 6 yards per move.

**Climb Speed**: It may climb up to 1.5 yards per move, presuming it is resonable terrain to do so.

**Swim speed**: It may swim up to 1 yard per move, presuming the water isn’t too rough.

**Unarmed Attacks**;

 • Claw: The velociraptor may use one of its claws to cut at a foe within 1 foot. This is a melee thrown blade attack.

 • Bite: The velociraptor may perform a single bite against a foe within 1 foot. This is a melee bite attack with a -1 penalty against armored foes or creatures of a larger size category than itself.

**Fleet Foot**: During the first round of an encounter, the velociraptor may use its action to perform a move.

**Tireless Stride**: If it did not sprint during the previous round, and is performing a sprint, it does not incur fatigue.

**Built for Speed**: When sprinting, its run speed is multiplied by the number of sprints it has performed since the start of the previous round.

**Keen Aural Senses**: When applying its [Prcp] bonus to checks/saves involving sense of hearing, it instead applies its [Prcp] score.

**Terrifying Leap**: Once per encounter, the velociraptor may quadruple its leap distance.

#### Gaining a Velociraptor Servile

An invoker which has earned the trust of a velociraptor can simply entreat it into their serivce as a lesser servile. When entreated into service this way, it is treated as having a single hitpoint.

**Dominion Benefits**: The velociraptor gains the benefit as appropriate to your domain.  
* **Chosen Spirit**. Anytime you receive spell sickness for any reason, the velociraptor knows your exact location for a number of rounds equal to the amount of spell sickness you received.
* **The Earth**. Bare and Toxic attacks against the volciraptor have disadvantage.
* **The Flame**. The first magical attack made against the velociraptor each round has disadvantage. If it was a cold or fire attack, it has no effect instead.
* **The Grave**. Necrotic and Ki attacks against the volciraptor have disadvantage.
* **The Light**: Radiant and Psychic attacks against the velociraptor have disadvantage.
* **Nobility**. It can determine if any sensory stimuli it receives is illusory. Its [Prcp] is increased by 1, increasing its bonus accordingly.
* **The Sea**. It can hold its breath twice as long as normal, and its swim speed is increased by .5 yard.
* **The Sky**. Its jump distance is doubled and its climb speed is increased by .5 yard.
* **Valdercaller**. It may treat its unarmed attacks as being *magical*. It gains a +1 to attacks against creatures you have successfully attacked this round.
