### Frimpiyr
A shapeshifting creature which feeds on books to acquire all knowledge contained therein. They can take the form of furniture or a figure that no one recognizes. They seem to enjoy well made, yet loose fitting, clothing.

**Creature Type**: Frimpiyrs are Eldritch creatures.

**Size**: In their typical human shape form, they are between 5'3-5'9, making them medium sized. They weigh 200 pounds.

**Hitpoints**: Frimpiyrs have 3 hitpoints.

**Attributes**:  
[Body] 10 (+3)  
[Fort] 9  (+3)  
[Rflx] 12 (+4)  
[Will] 17 (+5)  
[Inte] 20 (+6)  
[Prcp] 11 (+3)  
[Empy] 8  (+2) 

**Languages**: It does not speak, and its handwriting is illegible, but it knows a number of languages equal to its [Inte] bonus + 1.

**Run Speed**: It may run up to 4 yards per move.

**Climb Speed**: It may climb up to 4.5 yards per move, presuming it is reasonable terrain to do so.

**Swim Speed**: It may swim up to 1 yards, presuming the water isn't too rough.

**Unarmed Attacks**;

 • Strike: It may use any part of his body to strike a foe within 1 yard. This is a melee bare attack.

 • Claw: It may slash at foes with one of his forward claws within .5 yard. This is a melee cutting attack.

 • Bite: It may perform a single bite against a foe it is brawling with. This is a melee bite attack.

**Breathless**: A frimpiyr does not need to breathe.

**Some Knowledge**: It has proficiency in a random non-zoology skill and a random zoology skill.

**Book Eater**: The Frimpiyr conumes books whole to gain their knowledge. This takes 1 round for every half pound of the book. After a book is consumed, the creeature gains any benefit the book would provide and can instantly recall any information it contained. If it consumes a grimoire this way, it gains the ability to cast the contained spells at the tier indicated by the grimoire by wyrding.  
Every time a frimpiyr reads a completely new volume which contains a meaningful difference from all other books it has consumed, there is a % chance it gains a permanent increase to its [Inte] equal to the number of such books it has eaten since the previous time it would have received such a benefit.

**Tricky Form**: It can use an entire round to change its form from its humanshape form to that of some miscellaneous object no smaller than small size and no larger than medium size. It retains its own stats and features in either form, regardless of size. It must spend an entire round to change its form, during which time it is vulnerable.

**Spew**: Once per encounter, as an action, the frimpiyr may spew acid in a 1.5 yard semifan. This inflicts an acid attack. It smells awful.

**Unnatural Creeping**: When you it is prone, you do not suffer the usual penalty to movement.

**Natural Magic**: The frimpiyr is capable of casting wyrdings, such as those spells obtained from its Book Eater festure. It also knows a random basic spell and a random legendary spell. When casting a spell it knows, it gains a bonus to its spellcheck equal to its [Inte] bonus. It also gains this bonus for counterspell checks with such spells. When casting a spell obtained through its Book Eater feature, the bonus is instead equal to the number of different tiers available to it. It gains no bonus to counterspell checks for spells obtained through Book Eater.
