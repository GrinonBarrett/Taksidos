### Direcock
When a hen's egg is harbored by a venomous reptile, it can hatch into a terrifyingly dangerous creature. These creatures feature a similar frame to a rooster, but see their beak replaced with a round maw hiding a vortex of diminutive teeth

**Creature Type**: Direcocks are Avians.

**Size**: Direcocks stand between 9"-11". The weight is usually between 6 and 8 pounds. They are small creatures.

**Hitpoints**: A cat has no hitpoints.

**Attributes**:  
[Body] 6  (+2)  
[Fort] 8  (+2)  
[Rflx] 15 (+5)  
[Will] 9  (+3)  
[Inte] 9  (+3)  
[Prcp] 10 (+3)  
[Empy] 8  (+2)  

**Languages**: Direcocks don't speak, but can convey very basic information by crowing and hissing.

**Run Speed**: It may run up to 5.5 yards per move.

**Fly speed**: It may fly up to 3.5 yards per move, but cannot do so more than once per round.

**Unarmed Attacks**;

 • Scratch: The direcock may use its claw to strike a foe within 1 foot. This is a melee thrown blade attack.

 • Bite: The direcock may perform a single bite against a foe within 1 foot. This is a melee bite attack.

**Fleet Foot**: During the first round of an encounter, the cat may use its action to perform a move.

**Tireless Stride**: If it did not sprint during the previous round, and is performing a sprint, it does not incur fatigue.

**Terrifying Leap**: Once per encounter, the direcock may quadruple its leap distance.

**Skiddish**: During the first round of an encounter, when the cat first performs a reaction, roll a d4 and apply the appropriate result.  
1. No additional effect.
2. The cat does not perform the reaction, loses their reaction for the round, and is prone until the end of the next round.
3. The cat performs the reaction as normal but is prone until the end of the next round.
4. The reaction does not count against their reaction for the round.

**Venomous Spray**: Once per encounter, as an action or reaction, the direcock may spray a creature within a number of feet equal to 10 + [Fort]. This is a toxic attack. If the attack would qualify to damage armor, it instead causes the creature to perform a [Fort] save with a DV equal to the attack result. If the save is failed, the creature is prone for a number of rounds equal to the points of failure. If it is a critical failure, it is instead petrified during this time. If it is a critical success, it is immune to the same direcock's Venomous Spray.

#### Gaining a Direcock Servile

An invoker which has earned the trust of a direcock (which usually requires it imprint on the invoker upon hatching) can simply entreat it into their serivce as a lesser servile. When entreated into service this way, it is treated as having a single hitpoint.

**Dominion Benefits**: The cat gains the benefit as appropriate to your domain.  
* **Chosen Spirit**. The direcock rolls an additional d6 when performing a save against a magical effect.
* **The Earth**. The direcock gains a burrow speed of 1.5 yards.
* **The Flame**. The direcock is immune to fire damage.
* **The Grave**. Choose a number. Once this decision is made, it cannot be changed. Anytime the direcock's attack roll die result matches that number, it also inflicts a necrotic attack against the foe. It cannnot do so again from such necrotic attacks.
* **The Light**: It treats dim light as though it were fair light. You may choose to change the attack type of its Venomous Spray to Ki. If you do, it cannot be changed.
* **Nobility**. It gains a +2 bonus to defense against magical attacks.
* **The Sea**. Its gains a swim speed of 2 yards.
* **The Sky**. Its flight speed is increased by 1 yard.
* **Valdercaller**. The DV of its Venom Spray save is increased by 4. Its Run speed is increased by .5 yard.
