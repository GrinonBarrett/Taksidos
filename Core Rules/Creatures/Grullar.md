### Grullar
The frame of a pony, head of a boar, and tail of a fox make these grey haired beasts stand out in the wild. Though not predatory, they are extremely territorial. They will drive horses from their perceived territory and are often prey to creatures such as Alashanasaurus. The plural is grullain. Their collective noun is a herd.

**Creature Type**: Grullain are Beast creatures. 

**Size**: Grullain are between 4'3" to 5'1" at the withers. They can weigh between 500 and 900 pounds. They are medium creatures.

**Hitpoints**: A grullar has 1 hitpoint.

**Attributes**:  
[Body] 18 (+5)  
[Fort] 12 (+4)  
[Rflx] 13 (+4)  
[Will] 10 (+3)  
[Inte] 6 (+2)  
[Prcp] 11 (+3)  
[Empy] 7 (+2)  

**Languages**: Grullain don't speak.

**Run Speed**: It may run up to 7 yards per move.

**Swim Speed**: It may swim up to 2 yards per move, presuming the water isn't too rough.

**Unarmed Attacks**;

 • Slam: The grullar may use any part of its body to strike a foe within 1 yard. This is a melee bare attack.

 • Stomp: The grullar can attempt to stomp a foe, dealing a crushing attack. It has a -2 penalty to its attack, but has a +4 bonus for the purpose of determining if the attack is a critical hit against a foe of equal or smaller size.

 • Gore: If the grullar makes contact as a result of sprinting, it may use its tusk to gore the foe. This inflicts a piercing attack and does not require an action or reaction to perform this way.
