### Cragkin
Grandfolk of the mountains. The cragkin live in small settlements. Their societies embrace the voice of each member and liberal freedoms. In battle, they often charge recklesly. Their collective noun is a Clash.

**Creature Type**: Cragkin are Humanoids.

**Size**: Cragkin stand between 8" and 9'3" tall. They can weigh between 430-630 pounds. They are large creatures.

**Hitpoints**: Cragkin have 3 Hitpoints.

**Attributes**:  
[Body] 24 (+8)  
[Fort] 10 (+3)  
[Rflx] 12 (+4)  
[Will] 10 (+3)  
[Inte] 10 (+3)  
[Prcp] 10 (+3)  
[Empy] 10 (+3)  

**Languages**: Cragkin speak Jotine and another language as appropriate to where they operate.

**Run Speed**: It may run up to 6 yards per move.

**Climb Speed**: It may climb up to 2 yards per move, presuming it is reasonable terrain to climb.

**Swim speed**: It may swim up to 1.5 yards per move, presuming the water isn’t too rough.

**Weapon Attacks**: The cragkin is capable of using weapons. It even has proficiency in two weapons (usually hand axe and pole axe).

**Defenses**: A cragkin can use armor and a shield.

**Unarmed Attacks**;

 • Unarmed Strike: The cragkin may use part of its body to strike a foe within 2 feet. This is a melee bare attack.

#### Variants
Among the cragkin people, there are a few standouts. 

**Cragkin Warriors**: Cragkin warriors have additional training in weapons. Their normal weapon proficiencies are supplemented by proficiency with spear, hand axe, pike, greatsword, pole axe, mace, and maul.

**Cragkin Battleragers**: Cragkin battleragers are warriors who can enter a battlerage trance like a Berserk. They also have proficiency with hand axes, pole axes, spears, and pikes.

**Cragkin Cliffsingers**: Bards among the cragkin. Cliffsingers are capable of performing magic. They typically know the following spells: _Blast Lightning_, _Dragon's Breath_, _Lava Axe_, _Sun Globe_, and two more spells. Once per encounter, a cliffsinger may impose disadvantage on a wand or staff attack against itself.

--------

#### Special Features

**Show of Strength**: When the cragkin would gain a bonus to their melee attack due to having a higher [Body] score, they may choose to replace their +1 bonus to the attack with a bonus equal to their [Body] bonus. If they do, their foe adds their [Body] bonus to their defense.

**Cragkin Resilience**: Cragkin double their attribute bonus on all [Body] and [Will] saves against magical effects.
