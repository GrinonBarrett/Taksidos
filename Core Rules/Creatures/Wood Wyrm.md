### Wood Wyrm
Swift serpentine creatures which are capable of propelling themselves from tree to tree in their native woodland, these creatures can alter any ecp system. Thankfully, they prefer woodlands. Unfortunately, this often puts them at odds with local hunters and trappers. ALthough they do have two claws towards the front of their body, they are rather smaller and possess only 2 talons. They don't seem to use them in combat unless fighting with another wood wyrm over territory. Their collective noun is a slither.

**Creature Type**: Wood wyrms are Reptiles.

**Size**: Wood wyrms are between 12-15 feet in length. They are about 2.5 feet tall while crawling. They can weigh between 400 and 500 pounds. They are large creatures.

**Languages**: Wood wyrms don't speak, but click to communicate with others. To ward off a creature from its territory it will exhude a strong weezing sound while beating its tail on the ground.

**Hitpoints**: A wood wyrm has 2 hitpoints.

**Attributes**:  
[Body] 14 (+4)  
[Fort] 11 (+3)  
[Rflx] 13 (+4)  
[Will] 9 (+3)  
[Inte] 7 (+2)  
[Prcp] 14 (+4)  
[Empy] 6 (+2)  

**Run Speed**: It may run up to 8 yards per move.

**Climb Speed**: It may swim up to 3.5 yards per move.

**Swim Speed**: It may swim up to 2.5 yards per move, presuming the water isn't too rough.

**Burrow Speed**: It may burrow up to 1 yard per move presuming the earth is not too hard.  

**Unarmed Attacks**;

 • Smash: It may use any part of its body to strike a foe within 1.5 yards. This is a melee bare attack.

 • Tail: It may snap its tail at a foe within 1 yard of its rear. This is a melee club attack.

 • Bite: It can attempt to bite a foe within 2 feet, dealing a melee biting attack. It may only do so once per round.

**Spry**: The wood wyrm is proficienct in the Athletics and Wrestling skills.

**Keen Eye**: A wood Wyrm has a +3 to all checks and saves involving their eyesight.

**Tail Sweep**: Once per round, when its unarmed attack would be a critical hit or it slams a brawling opponent, it may use your reaction to do one of the following;

* It may have that creature perform a [Body] save with a DV equal to the wood wyrm's [Body] score plus its [Rflx] bonus. On a failure, that creature is knocked prone until the end of the round. On a critical failure, it is also vulnerable during this time.  
If it has a greater [Rflx] score than the wood wyrm, it gains advantage on the save.

* Inflict a Club attack against that creature.

**SHort Talons**: As an additional option when its succeeds a critical success while brawling, it may inflict either a cutting or piercing attack against that foe. If the attack would qualify to damage armor, it also inflicts an attack of the unselected type.

**Toxic Spray**: The wood wyrm can expel a potent combination of bile and venom against foes as an action. This is a 15 yard minifan which inflicts a toxic attack and acid attack against anything caught within the area. It may use its reaction to impose advantage on the attacks inflicted against a single foe within the area. It must rest before using this feature again.
