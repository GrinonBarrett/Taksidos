### Deep Lord
Ancient squid-like creatures which once ruled the oceans. The downfall of their empire predates all written record, but has set the course for the evolution of all form of life as we know it- including the migration of life onto the shores of land from the ocean. They include eight tentacles as part of their mantle, but appear to have two seperate appendages for which they prefer the use of. They have great patience except when dealing with sapiant creatures. Their motivations are yet to be understood by any sane mind.

**Creature Type**: Deep Lords are Undead.

**Size**: A deep lord is between 400 and 650 feet in height. They weigh between 10,000 and 18,000. They are big creatures.

**Undeath**: A deep lord has 2,000 points of undeath.

**Attributes**:  
[Body] 48 (+16)  
[Fort] 21 (+7)  
[Rflx] 14 (+4)  
[Will] 54 (+18)  
[Inte] 36 (+12)  
[Prcp] 30 (+10)  
[Empy] 9  (+3)  

**Languages**: Deep lords don't speak any language known to creatures other than themselves. They may understand a number of languages equal to their [Inte] bonus.

**Levitation Speed**: It may levitate up to 30 yards per move.

**Swim Speed**: It may swim up to 145 yards per move, presuming the water isn’t too rough.

**Climb Speed**: It may climb up to 40 yards per round, presuming it is reasonable terrain to do so.

**Unarmed Attacks**;

 • Bash: The deep lord may use one of its appendages to strike a foe within 50 yards. This is a melee force attack.

**Undead Nature**: As an undead creature, the deep lord has a few advantages:  
**-** They do not need to breathe, drink, eat, or sleep. Rest occurs when they are inactive.  
**-** Immunity to poisons, venoms, diseases, fatigue, bloodloss, and effects which instantly kill unless they specifically affect undead.  
**-** Cannot be incapacitated, except by an effect which explicitly affects undead.  
**-** Cannot be healed by an effect unless it specifically affects undead.  
**-** Cannot be destroyed by massive damage.  
**-** Does not produce warmth.  
**-** Does not have Hitpoints. Instead, it has points of undeath which must be exhausted before it is destroyed. Anytime an attack against this creature is successful, the creature loses a number of these points equal to the attack's total. Once the total is reduced to 0 or below, it is destroyed. A long rest restores half of the missing points of undeath. A full rest restores half again as many points of undeath as the long rest.

**Tough**: When an attack is inflicted against the deep lord, it calls a number. If the attack roll die result matches the called number, it does not lose any points of undeath as a result of the attack.

**Unnatural Resistance**: Until an attack has been successful against it during a round, *non-magical attacks have disadvantage against the deep lord*.

**Mighty Slam**: When the deep lord successfully strikes a foe with its bash attack, it may use its reaction to force that creature to succeed an opposed [Body] check. If the deep lord's check is a success in any degree, it knocks the foe prone and knocks them back a number of feet equal to the points of success mulitplied by the deep lord's weight in stones divided by the foe's weight in stones. If the deep lord had a critical success, the foe is also vulnerable while they are prone.

**Grapplock**: When performing an opposed check while brawling or grappling, the die level of any dice rolled by the deep lord is increased by 1. If an unarmed attack by this creature against a foe is a critical hit, and it has at least two free appendages, it may grapple that creature.

**Crusher**: If the deep lord's bash attack is successful against a creature of a smaller size category, it may choose to automatically grapple that creature until the end of the round. If it is grappling a creature, it gains advantage on its crush attack. Additionally, if that crush attack would be a critical success or qualify to damage armor- it inflict an additional such crush attack. This may occur multiple times as a result. 

**Spell Casting**: The deep lord is capable of casting spells as though by wyrding. It uses [Will] as its [Magic] attribute. It gains a bonus to its spellcheck and counterspell checks equal to its [Will] bonus. ANytime it casts a spell, it also receives an amount of spell sickness equal to half (round down) of the spell's tier. It knows all basic spells.
