### Dungeonling
Fiends born in abandoned structures which house magical artifacts. They have no purpose in their existence and will fight any non-fiend they encounter. Their collective noun is a dominion. 

**Creature Type**: Dungeonlings are fiends.

**Size**: They can stand between 2'6"-3'9'. They weigh between 50 and 80 pounds. They are small creatures.

**Languages**: Dungeonlings do not speak.

-----

**Hitpoints**: A dungeonling has 3 hitpoints.

**Attributes**:  
[Body] 12 (+4)  
[Fort]  9 (+3)  
[Rflx] 12 (+4)  
[Will] 10 (+3)  
[Inte]  6 (+2)  
[Prcp] 10 (+3)  
[Empy]  7 (+2)  

**Run Speed**: It may run up to 5 yards per move.

**Climbing Speed**: It may climb up to 4 yards per round, presuming it is reasonable terrain to do so.

**Swim Speed**: It may swim up to 3 yards per round, presuming the water isn't too rough.

**Unarmed Attacks**;

 • Claw: The dungeonling may strike a foe with its claws. This is a melee cuting attack.

#### Special Features

**Fiendish Nature**: The dungeonling's fiendish nature bestows it with a few particular features;  
 • Do not need to eat, sleep, or breathe. They do still need to relax to rest.  
 • Is not subject to bleeding.  
 • Is immune to diseases, poisons, and venom.  
 • Gains a bonus to all saves equal to current hitpoints.  
 • Is not affected by spells which would affect the flesh.   
 • Receives wounds, but no effect from them.

**Infernal Regeneration**: At the end of any round the dungeonling receives a wound and has any hitpoints remaining, it may use its reaction to roll a d8 and compare it to its current hitpoints. If the result is lower, it recovers a hitpoint. It must perform a rest before doing so again.

**Savage Jaws**: As an additional option when this creature gains a critical success when brawling, it can inflict 2 biting attacks against the foe.
